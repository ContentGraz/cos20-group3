I'm a big fan of the outdoor and clothing brand PATAGONIA. I also like their advertising approach as a best practice example. Toaday I'd like to share with you what I recently saw:

Patagonia's reversible poem

Patagonia, an american outdoor clothing brand, raised awareness for climate crisis with a simple print ad. The ad was printed in the The New York Times end of November 2020. This print ad is a creative approach to get across a brand's mission. Now, enjoy reading their peom:

We're all screwed
So don't tell us that
We can imagine a healthy future
Because the reality is
It's too late to fix the climate crisis
And we don't trust anyone who says
We need to demand a livable planet
Because we don't have a choice

(Now read this bottom up)
